# Credzaar

Credzaar is a gold loan clarity and comparison platform designed for Indian users.

## Problem

Gold loan users in India face:
- No transparency in interest rates
- Hidden charges
- Difficulty comparing lenders (banks vs NBFCs)
- Poor financial awareness

## Solution

Credzaar simplifies gold loans by:
- Comparing interest rates across lenders
- Showing total repayment clearly
- Educating users before they take loans
- Helping users make better financial decisions

## Target Users

- Middle-class households
- Small business owners
- People taking urgent loans against gold

## Features (MVP)

- Gold loan comparison dashboard
- Interest rate breakdown
- Lender listing (banks & NBFCs)
- Simple UI for non-tech users

## Future Vision

- AI-based loan recommendation
- Integration with lenders
- Instant loan approval
- Credit score-based suggestions

## Why I built this

I have experience running a grocery business and understanding real customer financial pain points.  
Credzaar is built to solve real problems in the Indian lending ecosystem.
